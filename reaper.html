Reaper

Here are some basic tips and tricks about using Reaper. (tested on Reaper 6.52)

  1. You can remove selection with ESC key.
  2. Pressing DELETE will delete the whole selected track.
  3. If you want to delete selected area of the track, press CTRL + DELETE.
  4. You can enable ripple editing (top left corner, second row, third column) to make track from the right move to the left.
  5. To change BPM of the project without affecting imported audio files, open Project Settings (ALT + ENTER, or in File menu in top bar) and change "Timebase for items/envelopes/markers" to Time. You can leave the second setting "Timebase for tempo/time signature envelope" as it is (Beats is default).
  6. To move track freely, without snapping to BPM grid, press SHIFT.
